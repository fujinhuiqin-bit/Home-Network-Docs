## 5. Ingredients (Inputs)

In our process documentation, ingredients are called Inputs. These are the resources we must have before we can start the baking process.

**Table 1. Cake Ingredients**

| Item | Details | Amount |
| :--- | :--- | ---: |
| Cake Flour | Sift it twice to make it smooth | 200 g |
| White Sugar | Must be clean and dry | 150 g |
| Eggs | Large size | 4 units |
| Whole Milk | Fresh milk | 50 ml |
| Unsalted Butter | Melt it into a liquid first | 40 g |

### Preparation Pre-requisites
To ensure we get consistent quality every time, we must follow these 3 rules before mixing:

* **Clean Tools:** Confirm that all mixing bowls are dry and oil-free.
* **Sift Flour:** Sift the flour to make sure there are no lumps in the batter.
* **Temperature:** Keep the eggs and milk at room temperature, which is about 20°C.
